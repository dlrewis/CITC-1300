High Speed Rail

What is High Speed Rail?

High Speed Rail (or HSR for short) refers to rail lines which are significantly faster than regular rail lines.

Where is High Speed Rail used?

As of 2024, China is currently, by far, the country with the most HSR lines. It has a total of 26,869 km of rail, which is 23,769 km longer than than that of Spain, which has the second largest amount of HSR lines.

  • China - 26,869 km
  • Spain - 3,100 km
  • Japan - 3,041 km
  • France - 3,220 km
  • Germany - 3,038 km
